Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment

Cut Force Outdoor Services is committed to providing equal access to our services for all customers, including those with disabilities. We strive to meet the accessibility standards outlined in the Accessible British Columbia Act.

Website Accessibility

This website follows Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1 Level AA) where possible, including:

  • Skip links for keyboard navigation
  • Semantic HTML structure for screen readers
  • Descriptive alt text for images
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios
  • Resizable text without loss of functionality
  • Clear heading structure and focus indicators

Service Accessibility

We are committed to accommodating customers with disabilities:

  • Communication: We offer phone, text, and email options. If you need alternative communication methods, please let us know.
  • Site access: We will work with you to assess accessibility needs at your property. Please inform us of any specific requirements when booking.
  • Service modifications: Where reasonable, we will adjust our approach to accommodate accessibility needs.
