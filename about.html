About Our Team

Our Story

Cut Force Outdoor Services was founded in Hope, BC with a simple mission: to provide reliable, professional outdoor property recovery and cleaning services that restore neglected spaces to their natural beauty. We understand the unique challenges of outdoor property maintenance in the Pacific Northwest.

Our team combines industrial-level equipment with careful attention to your property's specific needs. From precise chainsaw work to thorough pressure washing, we approach every job with respect for both your land and the surrounding environment.

Our Mission

We bring power and precision to outdoor cleanup and restoration. Every property deserves a second chance, and every client deserves professional results that last.

Reliability

We show up on time, every time. Rain or shine, we deliver consistent, professional service.

Safety

Certified operators and proper safety protocols ensure your property and family are protected.

Respect for Land

We work with nature, not against it. Our approach minimizes environmental impact while maximizing results.

Property Restoration

From storm damage to years of neglect, we restore outdoor spaces to their full potential.
