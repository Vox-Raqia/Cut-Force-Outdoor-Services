Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your location/service area
  • Description of services needed
  • Optional photos you choose to share

How We Use Your Information

We use this information solely to:

  • Provide you with a quote for our services
  • Schedule and complete requested work
  • Communicate with you about your service request

Analytics & Tracking Technologies

We partner with Microsoft Clarity and Microsoft Advertising to capture how you use and interact with our website through behavioral metrics, heatmaps, and session replay to improve and market our products/services. Website usage data is captured using first and third-party cookies and other tracking technologies to determine the popularity of products/services and online activity. Additionally, we use this information for site optimization, fraud/security purposes, and advertising. For more information about how Microsoft collects and uses your data, visit the Microsoft Privacy Statement.

Cookies

We use cookies to manage your preferences and for analytics. You can control cookie settings through the banner on our site or by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer. Cookies are used to:

  • Remember your consent preferences
  • Analyze site usage to improve user experience

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as necessary to provide quotes and complete services. Analytics data is retained according to Microsoft Clarity's data retention policies.

Your Rights (CCPA/PIPEDA)

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal information from our records
  • Ask questions about how your data is used
  • Contact us to update or remove your information
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell personal data)

Do Not Track

We honor Do Not Track signals where technically feasible. You may also opt out of tracking by selecting "Essential Only" in our cookie consent banner.

Third-Party Services

We use FormSubmit.co for form processing. We recommend reviewing their privacy policy for details on their data handling practices.
