What Helps Me Give You a Fast, Accurate Quote

A little information up front makes communication easier and speeds up the quoting process.

  • Photos of the area — wide shots and close‑ups help me understand the scope.
  • Location details — address, neighbourhood, or general area.
  • Access info — gates, driveways, steep terrain, tight spaces, or anything unusual.
  • Hazards to be aware of — pets, wasps, unstable structures, overgrowth, etc.
  • Scope of work — what you want done, what’s urgent, and what’s optional.
  • Timeline — if you need it done soon or are flexible.

You can send all of this by text or email — whatever’s easiest.
