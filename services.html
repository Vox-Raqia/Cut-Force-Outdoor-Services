Our Services

Practical outdoor work for real properties in Hope, BC

Chainsaw Work

Small-to-medium tree and limb work done safely

This includes:

  • Cutting and removing small trees
  • Limb removal
  • Clearing fallen or problem branches
  • Improving safety around structures where it's safe to do so

If a job requires rigging, climbing, or advanced arborist equipment, you'll be told upfront — no guessing, no risks.

Brush Clearing

Reduce fire risk and reclaim overgrown areas

Brush clearing helps:

  • Remove dry, overgrown vegetation
  • Reduce wildfire risk
  • Open up pathways and usable space
  • Improve visibility and property access

This is practical, safety-focused work that makes your property easier to maintain.

Pressure Washing

Clean, restore, and refresh exterior surfaces

Pressure washing is ideal for:

  • Driveways
  • Walkways
  • Decks and patios
  • Siding and exterior walls
  • Moss, grime, and buildup removal

A clean surface makes a huge difference — especially in our damp BC climate.
